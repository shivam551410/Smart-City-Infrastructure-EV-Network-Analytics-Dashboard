# ⚡ Smart City Infrastructure & EV Network Digital Twin Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Smart City Infrastructure & EV Network Digital Twin Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using **Microsoft Power BI** to monitor and analyze Electric Vehicle (EV) infrastructure, charging station performance, energy consumption, environmental sustainability, and urban mobility.

This dashboard provides real-time insights into EV adoption, charging station utilization, renewable energy usage, traffic congestion, and carbon emission reduction, enabling city planners and policymakers to make informed decisions for building sustainable smart cities.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

With the rapid growth of electric vehicles, cities need a centralized analytics platform to monitor EV infrastructure, optimize charging networks, reduce carbon emissions, and improve urban transportation efficiency.

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

- Monitor EV infrastructure performance
- Analyze charging station utilization
- Track renewable and grid energy consumption
- Measure environmental impact through CO₂ reduction
- Optimize charging station deployment
- Support sustainable urban planning

# 🚀 Dashboard Features

## 🚗 EV Infrastructure Overview

Monitor the overall performance of the city's EV ecosystem through interactive KPI cards displaying:

- Total Registered EVs
- Charging Stations
- Charging Sessions
- Energy Consumption
- Revenue Generated
- CO₂ Saved

---

## 🗺️ EV Charging Station Map

Interactive geographical visualization showing:

- Charging station locations
- AC Charger Distribution
- DC Fast Charger Distribution
- City-wise infrastructure coverage

**Business Value**

Helps identify underserved areas and optimize future charging station installations.

---

## 📈 Charging Session Trend

Visualizes daily charging sessions over time to identify:

- Peak charging days
- Usage trends
- Infrastructure demand

---

## ⚡ Energy Consumption Analysis

Compare monthly:

- Grid Energy Consumption
- Renewable Energy Consumption

to evaluate energy efficiency and sustainability goals.

---

## 🌿 Energy Source Breakdown

Displays the percentage contribution of:

- Renewable Energy
- Grid Energy

This enables decision-makers to monitor clean energy adoption.

---

## 🌍 CO₂ Savings Analysis

Track daily carbon emission reduction achieved through EV usage.

Provides insight into:

- Environmental impact
- Sustainability progress
- Green energy initiatives

## 📊 EV Growth Analysis

Shows daily EV growth percentage, allowing authorities to monitor adoption trends and forecast future infrastructure requirements.

Analyze:
